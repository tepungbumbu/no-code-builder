## How to Use

### Option 1: Auto-Login (Easiest)
1. Just refresh the page: `http://localhost:3000`
2. You'll be automatically logged in as the demo user
3. Start creating projects!

### Option 2: Manual Login
1. Visit `http://localhost:3000/login`
2. Enter:
   - Email: `demo@example.com`
   - Password: `password123`
3. Click "Login"

### Option 3: Create Your Own Account
1. Visit `http://localhost:3000/register`
2. Enter any email/password
3. Account will be created (stored in memory)
