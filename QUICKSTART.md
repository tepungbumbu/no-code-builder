## üéÆ How to Play Around

Once you are in, here is what you can do:

### 1. Open a Project
You will see a project called **"My First Website"**. Click the **"Edit"** button (pencil icon) to open it.

### 2. The Editor Screen
This is where the magic happens.
- **Left Side**: Your toolbox. Drag things like Buttons, Text, or Images from here into the center.
- **Center**: The Canvas. This is your website page. Drop items here!
- **Right Side**: The Settings. Click on any item in the canvas to change its color, text, or size here.

### 3. Make Changes
- Try dragging a **Button** onto the page.
- Click the button, then change its text in the Right Panel.
- Try changing the view to **Mobile** (click the phone icon at the top) to see how it looks.

## ‚ùì Common Questions

**Q: Do I need to save my work?**
A: No! The app auto-saves everything you do.

**Q: Can I create a new project?**
A: Yes! Go back to the Dashboard and click "Create New Project".

**Q: Is this a real website on the internet?**
A: Not yet. Right now it's running locally on your computer. To put it on the internet, you would need to "Deploy" it (which is a more advanced topic).

**Q: I broke something! How do I reset?**
A: Since this is a demo running in memory, just restart the terminal command (`npm run dev`), and it might reset some data depending on how it's saved. Or just delete the project and make a new one!
